  <p><strong>A note about the performance</strong></p>  <p>Windows 7 runs good on my machine and boot fast, but I don&#180;t know if it is faster than a clean &quot;Vista&quot;, but I really hope that the performance is much better than with Vista.</p>  <p><strong>Is it stable?      <br /></strong>I played around for 2 hours with Windows 7 and drag some gadgets, explore the taskbar and so on.
